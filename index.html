About Me

We are a conservation-oriented training company focusing on skills development of field staff in wildlife protected areas in Southern Africa. Successful wildlife management business is depended upon an effective and competent game rangers, scouts, or game guard teams. Skills development for field staff is critical for wildlife survival and sustainable resources utilisation. We specialise in field training and ecological assessments of wildlife protected areas. We offer both on-site and off-site training. Wildlife crime is a significant threat to the success of all protected areas in the region, and our training focus to address this challenge.
Our trainers and Assessors are current practising professionals in the field who shares and guide our students furnishing them with current poaching trends and other wildlife related crimes, which help focus our training and implement modern anti-poaching techniques.
We are highly mobile and can train field rangers all over Zimbabwe and beyond our borders. We also offer continued support and management of Anti-poaching units remotely and offer supervisory services.

Courses Offered

 Basic Game Ranger Course: 21 days

• The course is offered as either onsite or offsite to the Ranger’s place of employment.
• The course targets newly employed field rangers/ game guards or scouts and those already in the field requiring professional training.
• A physically demanding anti-poaching training course which covers patrol techniques, wildlife monitoring, wilderness survival skills and navigation, wildlife monitoring, Crime scene management, conservation ethics among other modules.
• 40% theory and 60% practical. Accommodation in tents or anything provided.
• A minimum of 10 and maximum of 20 people at a time for the course. 

 Advanced Game Ranger Course: 10 days

• The course is tactical focusing more on patrol leadership, protected area security operation planning, informer network and information management, Access control, Contact and Cover, Search and Arrest Procedures, Night Operations etc.
• Course preferable to be presented on site in the ranger’s area of operation
• Minimum 10 and maximum 20 people 

 Counter insurgence Tracker Training course: 10 days 

• A very tactical tracking course for game Rangers.
• Physically and mentally demanding course
• Course modules outline include aggressive human tracking, fire and movements, self-defence field techniques, camouflage and concealment, Spoor identification and reporting procedure etc.
• Course presented on and off site.
• Minimum 6 and maximum 10 

 Tracking course: 7 days course

This tracking course was developed to meet the needs of Game rangers, Field rangers/Game scouts/Game guards, Field guides and trackers in general. It is also suitable for nature lovers who want to enjoy Africa on foot and learn all the wonders and signs, of the untamed Africa.

Modules will include:
• Introduction to tracking
• Principles of tracking and trailing
• Human and wildlife tracking
• Animal behaviour, track and sign identification
• Advanced tracking for anti-poaching and security
• Tracking and wildlife monitoring.
• The use of tracking devices and camera trapping.
• Targeting the hunting and guiding industry for guides and trackers.
• Ideal for nature enthusiast.
• Course presented on and off site.
• Minimum 6 and maximum is 10
